## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Is UNIVRS an agency?

No. UNIVRS is a senior-led consulting practice. Engagements are intentional, limited, and deeply involved.

---

### Do you build software?

Sometimes. But only when it supports clarity. UNIVRS prioritises **thinking, structure, and guidance** before code.

---

### Do you replace internal teams?

No. UNIVRS strengthens teams, mentors leaders, and designs systems teams can own confidently.

---

### Is this AI consulting?

Partially. UNIVRS focuses on **AI-augmented delivery**, not AI hype or model-chasing.

---

### What makes UNIVRS different?

Experience, restraint, and honesty.  
UNIVRS will tell you **what not to build** as readily as what to build.

---

### How do engagements start?

Usually with a conversation. No pressure. No sales theatre. Just clarity.
